Modest Fashion

There is not one definition of what modest fashion means, but it essentially relates to having a degree of awareness when it comes to covering up parts of your body. So, in brief, modest fashion can describe varying degrees of covering up on purpose. The decision can be due to religious/ethnic fulfillment or to attain a certain aesthetic and level of ease because it is not just a trend that's tied to spirituality. It's a hotly debated topic, and like with anything in this world, there are many sides to the story.
